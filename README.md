# Case study — Theresas Städ

How [Theresas Städ](https://theresasstad.se) — a familjeägt and kvinnodrivet städföretag in Göteborg with 20+ års erfarenhet — got a streamlined website built and maintained by [Webraketen](https://webraketen.se).

## Stack

- **Astro** for SEO-critical pages
- **Netlify** for hosting + form functions
- **Resend** for transactional emails

## What Theresas Städ does

Familjeägt och kvinnodrivet städföretag i Göteborg med över 20 års erfarenhet. Hemstäd, flyttstäd, kontorsstäd. RUT-avdrag, F-skatt, kollektivavtal.

→ [theresasstad.se](https://theresasstad.se)

## Brand position

Personal — Theresa is the founder, the brand carries her name. The site reflects that: warmer copy, photos of real personnel, emphasis on long-term customer relationships.
